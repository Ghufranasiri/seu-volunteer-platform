About this Opportunity

This is a wonderful opportunity to make a direct impact on children's lives by helping them improve their English language skills. We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers who are passionate about education and working with children.

You will be working with underprivileged kids aged 8-12, providing personalized tutoring sessions, helping with homework, and creating an encouraging learning environment. No prior teaching experience is required, just a genuine love for education!

Session times are flexible and can be scheduled according to your availability. You'll receive all necessary materials and training from our experienced coordinators.

Requirements & Skills
Required Skills:

  • Communication
  • Patience
Preferred Qualifications:
  • Prior tutoring experience
  • Experience with group activities
  • Knowledge of teaching methods
